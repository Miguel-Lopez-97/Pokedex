VISUAL SPECIFICATION: Higher or Lower Game

1. Layout:
   - Centered container.
   - Top: "Score" and "Remaining" counters in a dark, rounded box with gold border.
   - Middle: Two large cards side-by-side (flex row).
     - Left Card: Fully visible Pokémon details (Image, Name, Type, Stats). This is the "Base" card.
     - Center: A red "VS" circle with white text, overlapping the space between cards.
     - Right Card: Similar structure to Left Card, but the target stat is hidden (shows "???") until guessed.
   - Overlay (Controls):
     - When playing, a control box overlays the Right Card (or sits between them on larger screens if desired, but currently implemented as overlay/center).
     - Buttons: "Higher" (Green) and "Lower" (Red).
   - Bottom: Result feedback text (Correct/Wrong) appearing large and centered.

2. Colors & Theme:
   - Background: Light (white/gray).
   - Cards: White with shadow.
   - Buttons: Vibrant Green/Red for actions, Yellow/Blue for branding.
   - Font: Segoe UI / Sans-serif.
